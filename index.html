Hey stranger,

every day new challenges, new experiences, coffee shops after work and alarm for the next morning. Faces, duties, notifications, dreamings about a vacation… And there is something more meaningful behind. Behind this lifestyle as an answer there is a question.

It’s about your definition.
Who do you want to be tomorrow?
What are you willing to give?

There are two wolves and they are always fighting. One is consumeristic and only wants to possess and enjoy, unhappy inside, the other is altruist and nobel and thus happy inside.
Which one wins?
The one you feed.
Make a choice today.

For yourself

  • believe in yourself
  • trust the process
  • open your heart
  • invest in yourself
  • be thankful
  • no complains
  • no regrets (everything happens for a reason)

For others

  • smile to the stranger
  • buy a coffee for someone listen more, talk less
  • text someone you care about
  • open up to people
  • don’t take things for granted

For the universe

  • plant a tree
  • choose things you can use more than once
  • respect other living things learn more about
  • eco-living and farming

Just think...
